Something About Us

Everybody is very much aware that today’s world is of cut throat competition & rat race. Today’s children have to go through it. Parents seem to have concern about it but at the same time they want their child to excel in academics as well as extracurricular activities along with their own expectations. And thus, children are forced automatically to join this race & run beyond their capacity. But at the same time parents & we as society forget that children have their own liking, abilities & capacities. These things or their other skills need to be found out to build their confidence & self image. Unless these are strong, children cannot groom themselves & develop other life skills. Today is the world where children have to be ready with or they need to inculcate: Physical Strength Knowledge Logical Acumen Emotional Stability Self-Belief Persistence Love Spiritual Connect MASTER MIND introduces in here itself to help & support the children to inculcate these qualities & in finding out their areas of interest & capabilities which eventually will boost up their confidence & self image. Parents & schools & institutions teach academics & support in other activities but there is no proper grooming as such. MASTER MIND aims to do it. And for that it plans to conduct some activities like quiz, drawing competitions, elocution competition & other cultural activities like dance, drama, singing and sports. MASTER MIND will also conduct IQ & Aptitude tests for children as & when required to find out their areas of interest & skills that need to be honed. We will also inform children about different scholarships children can avail & various competitive exams. Our working area would be rural, semi urban & urban. Initially these activities will be conducted in schools & then will be brought on a bigger platform. MASTER MIND will arrange for guidance from experts & professionals in the field. The activities will be tailored & customised as per need and special attention will be given that it develops both creative & critical thinking in children.

Objectives

These objectives define what we do and what we are trying to

Scholarships

To spread awareness about scholarships from Govt, Private & International sectors.

Guidance

To encourage children to go ahead & participate in the activities which are their strong areas and help them to identify the same.

Testing

To arrange various IQ, EQ and other intelligence and personality tests to identify the personality traits.

Platform

To provide a primary platform to children to find out their areas of interest by organizing various activities.

Grooming

To groom children & provide them a larger canvas at city level, district level & state level. Also at National televisions.

training

Special training facilities for star achiever students.
